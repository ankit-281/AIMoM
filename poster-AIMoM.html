AIMoM

AIoOM: AI-Powered Meeting Assistant

Real-time transcription, structured summaries, and automatic visual mind maps
Conversational AI Systems Project
Abstract
AIMoM converts live meeting conversations into structured outputs while the discussion is still happening. It captures the spoken stream, transcribes in real time, summarizes context continuously, and delivers a final visual mind map to improve recall and decision tracking.
Problem Statement
Teams frequently lose critical decisions and action items in long discussions. Manual note-taking is inconsistent, error-prone, and often misses discussion structure.
Goal: automate meeting memory without interrupting the meeting flow.

AIMoM End-to-End Pipeline
Layer 1: Audio and Speech Intelligence
Continuous microphone feed is segmented and processed with OpenAI Whisper for real-time ASR. Optional speaker diarization tags segments by participant identity when enabled.
Streaming Audio Whisper ASR Diarization Timestamps
Layer 2: Local Summarization Engine
A fine-tuned local LLM consumes transcript windows and generates concise structured summaries during the live discussion, preserving context while minimizing latency.
Local LLM Prompt Template Rolling Context Low Latency
Layer 3: Visual Knowledge Output
At meeting end, the system transforms final structured notes into Mermaid syntax and renders a visual mind map for instant review and post-meeting knowledge transfer.
Mermaid Graph Topic Nodes Action Branches Export Ready
Impact Statement
AIMoM acts as a persistent meeting intelligence layer that translates raw conversation into actionable organizational memory.

Why AIMoM Improves Productivity
AIMoM reduces cognitive load by removing manual note burden, keeps all participants aligned on decisions, and creates an immediately actionable record of discussion flow. Teams spend less time reconstructing meetings and more time executing outcomes.
